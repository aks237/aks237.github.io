Kinematic Machine

As part of my mechanical synthesis class, I designed a precise CAD model of an Exact Slider Crank Ellipse and Straight-Line Linkage, inspired by a physical model displayed in our engineering school. This project required a thorough study of the linkage's kinematics to ensure accurate replication of its motion. The linkage was particularly fascinating due to its ability to generate straight-line and elliptical paths, showcasing the elegance of mechanical design. Through this project, I honed my skills in both CAD modeling and motion analysis, while gaining a deeper appreciation for the ingenuity and historical significance of classic linkage mechanisms in engineering.
